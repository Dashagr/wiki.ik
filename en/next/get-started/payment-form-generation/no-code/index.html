No code

If you do not use the website, but sell goods or provide services through alternative ways (messengers, Instagram, Telegram), you can generate a link or QR code with a link to the Interkassa payment page and send it to payers.

You can see exactly what the payment process looks like from the buyer's point of view by following this link.

To generate a payment form and start receiving payments, you need:

Step 1. Find the checkout and go to payment the form generation page​

In your account, go to the My checkouts page. Select the checkout for which you need to generate a payment form and click on the special 'Create bill' icon.

'Create bill' icon

Step 2. Fill in the required fields​

Now you are on the invoice creation page. In the fields of the form, specify:

  • payment number to make it easier to search for it in the system,
  • the date until which the account will be valid;
  • the number of payments that can be made using this link;
  • addresses of payers (optional);
  • amount and currency of the account;
  • description of the account (for example, for which product the payer will pay).

Creating a form for payments

Also, when creating an account, you can specify the URL of the interaction for successful and unsuccessful payment and we will send a callback to this address as soon as the payment is made.

Creating a form for payments

Step 3. Create a payment form​

After all the data is filled, click on the 'Create' button.

Done! Now you can copy the link or the generated QR code and send it to payers by mail, SMS, or in messenger. After the link is pressed, a buyer will be taken to the Interkassa payment page where he can pay conveniently. The QR code is suitable for both online and offline payments.

tip

You can also add a logo and a short description of your market to the payment page. Detailed instructions are available here.

Generated link and QR code for payment

Issued invoices have different statuses from the moment of creation to the moment of payment. You can track your invoice status on the Accepted Payments page. For more information on tracking payments, see the Managing Payments section.
