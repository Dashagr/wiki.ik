Manage transfers

Information about all transfers is located on the Transfers page in the Interkassa portal (from the top menu Operations -> Transfers). On this page, you can create a new transfer and view the history of all transfers sorted by id.

Main page of the Transfers section

Transfer Parameters

Field NameDescription
idunique transfer number
Datetransfer creation date
Amounttransfer amount. Positive value means the transfer came to your account, negative value means the transfer was sent from your account
Order IDInterkassa system transfer id (it must be unique within your wallet. You can specify it yourself when working through the API)
Sendersender wallet id
Receiverrecipient wallet id
Operation Typetransfer type. Possible values:
  • transfer – regular transfer;
  • system replenishment – funds credited from the payment system to your wallet
  • system debit – withdrawal of funds by the system from your wallet to the account of the payment system.
Statusthe current status of the transfer. Possible values:
  • new – the transfer has been formed, but not yet carried out;
  • successful – the transfer was successful and the money was credited to your checkout;
  • canceled – transfer canceled.
comment to the regular or system money transfer
Take note

It is important for merchants to track all their transactions for record keeping and accounting purposes. Therefore, we recommend export data about transfers from your bank account regularly. This will provide a complete overview of all your transactions and make it easier to manage your finances
