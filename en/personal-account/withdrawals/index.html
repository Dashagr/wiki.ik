Withdrawals

Withdrawal money operation is withdrawing money from your account at the Interkassa checkout to your external resource (the bank card / crypto wallet / mobile account, etc).

Withdrawal creation​

To withdraw funds, you need:

Step 1. Payment page​

Go to the My checkouts tab, find the chechout from which you want to withdraw money, and click on the special 'Withdraw' icon.

'Withdraw' icon

Or press the 'Create' button on Withdrawals page.

'Create' button

Step 2. Form filling​

On the next page, enter the amount you want to withdraw, or click the 'all' checkbox to select the entire available cash balance amount. From the drop-down list on the left, choose a payment system for payment.

Creating a withdrawal

Attention!
If you are withdrawing funds to a PrivatBank card, don't forget to indicate this in the drop-down list in the 'Amount to receive' field.
To withdraw your funds in USDT, check the drop-down list in the 'Amount to receive' field. Specify:

USDTTRC for TRC 20 network,
USDTERC for ERC 20 network

Next to the 'Amount to receive' field, you can click on the 'more info' button and view withdrawal details, including fees from the payment system.

Withdrawal details

Check the amount to be received and click 'Create Withdrawal'.

Step 3. Data check​

On the next screen, check the data again and make sure that all the information is correct. If you notice an error, you can change the data by clicking on the appropriate button.

Data correctness check screen

Step 4. Withdrawal creation​

Click on the 'Create operation' button.

Congratulations! You have successfully created a withdrawal request.

Notification of payment creation

You can see that the withdrawal has taken place and follow the status of the withdrawal on the Withdrawals tab in the top menu.

tip

You can also export data on withdrawals or payments in excel format from the My checkouts page.

FAQ​

There is no required payment system in the drop-down list at my checkout.
In this case, contact your account manager or write to the Interkassa support service atsupport@interkassa.com, нour specialists will connect the appropriate payment system.

What to do next?​

📄️ Managing withdrawals

Information about all payments and withdrawals is available on the Withdrawals page.

📄️ Withdrawal by a registry

Withdrawal by a registry or multi-withdrawals are an effective tool for optimizing your business. Use the register payments function and pay out funds for several details at the same time.
